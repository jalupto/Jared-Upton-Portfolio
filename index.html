Well hello there! As you can see, my name is Jared, and I'm a junior developer. In fact, I just started coding in March of 2021. Before that, I had no coding experience whatsoever. What I do have though is adaptability, strong attention to detail considering I'm a perfectionist, and an eye for design since I did professional photography for six years as well. I was led me to coding after talking to a career counselor and taking the career assessment to see what lines up with my personality, because everything else before just didn't seem to fit. This field was my top match, and from what I've seen so far, it is extremely accurate and I'm grateful to have finally found the job that I can be passionate about and enjoy for the long run.

ABOUT ME

My ultimate goal is to provide a better future for my family. I have a wife and one year old son who I both cherish. This career will be the dream I've had for so long.
